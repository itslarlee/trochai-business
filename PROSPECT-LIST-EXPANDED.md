## Notes

- **WhatsApp prevalence**: Nearly all agencies in Costa Rica use WhatsApp as their primary communication channel with buyers, especially for international clients. Beach zone agencies have even higher WhatsApp dependence due to time zone differences with North American/European buyers.
- **Instagram activity**: Agencies with active Instagram presence (CasaMax, Jaco Real Estate CR, Nosara Real Estate Agency, CRRES Dominical) are more likely to be tech-forward and open to adopting Trochai.
- **Franchise vs. independent**: Independent/boutique agencies are better first targets (faster decision-making, owner feels the WhatsApp pain directly). Franchise offices (Century 21, RE/MAX) can be approached later -- the local owner/broker makes the tool adoption decision.
- **CCCBR membership**: Agencies that are CCCBR (Camara Costarricense de Corredores de Bienes Raices) members tend to be more professionalized and open to tools that improve their service quality.
