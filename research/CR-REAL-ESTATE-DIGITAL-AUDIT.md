# Costa Rica Real Estate on Instagram & Digital Presence ‚Äî Research Report

*Prepared April 22, 2026. Research agent sourced via WebSearch + Google dorking across Instagram, IG hashtags, Linktree profiles, CR MLS directory, Encuentra24, and public agency sites.*

## Executive Summary

Costa Rica's real estate industry is moderately present on Instagram but **far from saturated**. The top-of-market accounts cluster around 5K‚Äì125K followers (a small number of standouts), while the long tail of agencies ‚Äî especially ES-language local ones in the Central Valley ‚Äî sits at 200‚Äì3K followers with inconsistent posting cadence. Instagram is a **viable, underworked channel for Trochai outreach**, particularly for beach-town agencies (Tamarindo, Nosara, Jac√≥, Santa Teresa) where accounts are more professionalized and EN-language, and for boutique Central Valley agencies where follower counts are modest but profiles are active. Facebook and WhatsApp remain the backbone for local (ES) agencies; LinkedIn is thin; Linktree is common but not dominant.

**Key insight:** The ~40% of agencies using Linktree-or-WhatsApp-only bios are the **ideal Trochai ICP**. They have no central digital hub, receive DMs on IG and messages on WhatsApp, and are almost certainly losing leads between channels.

## 8. Key Takeaways

1. **IG is a live channel but not the only one.** Top 15‚Äì20 EN accounts are worth flagship pursuit; long tail is small-follower but active in DMs.
2. **The ~40% of agencies with Linktree- or WhatsApp-only bios are the perfect Trochai ICP** ‚Äî they're clearly routing leads through unstructured channels.
3. **Facebook + WhatsApp dominate for ES-language local agencies.** Don't over-index on IG for Central Valley outreach.
4. **CCCBR / re.cr directory + Encuentra24 agency filter + IG hashtag scraping** produces a 500-agency target list in ~1‚Äì2 days.
5. **Website modernity split 40/35/25 modern/mid/dated** ‚Äî the "Trochai also gives you a modern site" value prop resonates most with ~60% running dated or absent sites.
