# LinkedIn Post 1 — Week of 2026-04-07

97% of real estate leads are wasted.

Not because they weren't interested. Because nobody responded fast enough.

I've been building Trochai — an AI-powered WhatsApp inbox for real estate agencies in Costa Rica — and the data behind lead loss is staggering:

→ 78% of buyers work with the first agent who responds (NAR, 2025)
→ 62% of inquiries arrive outside business hours
→ 48% of agents never follow up after the first contact
→ 80% of sales require 5+ interactions to close

The pattern is clear: leads come in, but agencies can't process them at the speed buyers expect.

The agencies that convert above average all share three things:
1. Instant first response (even if automated)
2. Structured follow-up process
3. After-hours coverage

This isn't a marketing problem. It's an operations problem. And it's solvable.

What's the average response time at your agency?
