# Walking Reel Variations

**Format:** Elegant woman walking with coffee (POV/hip-level shot, Veo 3 generated), serif text overlay, trending audio. Same base video for all — just swap the text in CapCut or Instagram Edits.

**Audio suggestion:** Tame Impala - Dracula (from the reference), or whatever's trending that week.

**Style:** All on-screen text follows the "Quien dijo que... se equivocó. Lea el caption." format. The hook is on screen, the data/proof is in the caption. Forces people to tap "more" = boosts engagement metrics.
