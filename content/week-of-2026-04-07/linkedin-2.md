# LinkedIn Post 2 — Week of 2026-04-07

I tested something with real estate agencies in Costa Rica.

I sent WhatsApp inquiries to 10 agencies on a Tuesday at 8pm. Asked about a 2-bedroom apartment in Escazú. Simple question, clear intent.

Results:
→ 3 responded that night (all within 2 hours)
→ 4 responded the next morning (12+ hours later)
→ 2 responded 2 days later
→ 1 never responded

The 3 that responded same-night? All had some form of automation — auto-reply, chatbot, or a dedicated night-shift agent.

The other 7 were running the same playbook most agencies run: "we'll get to it tomorrow."

In a market where 78% of buyers choose the first responder, "tomorrow" is too late.

This is why I'm building Trochai. Not to replace agents, but to make sure the lead is already engaged when the agent picks up the conversation the next morning.

The 5-minute window is real. Are you covering it?
