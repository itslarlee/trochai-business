Spent the morning reading Microsoft's latest study on Costa Rican SMEs.

53% advancing rapidly in digital transformation. 50% already using AI in daily ops. CR ranks 5th in LATAM for AI readiness.

The exception inside that data — and it's not subtle — is real estate. Most agencies I talk to here still maintain their inventory across five+ disconnected places: an internal MLS, the national portals, their own WordPress site, the WhatsApp catalog, Instagram captions. Editing one property = touching all five. Which means at least one is always wrong.

The wedge isn't "agencies are behind." It's that the architecture asked for the impossible. Five sources of truth = no source of truth.
