Sunday founder note (full piece on Trochai's company page in Spanish).

When we started building Trochai, I assumed real estate agencies wanted to replace WhatsApp Business with a centralized web inbox. Q1 discovery calls said otherwise.

What agents actually don't want to give up: voice and video calls with clients, group chats with families/developers, and the phone-in-hand speed. Migrating to a web inbox asks them to renounce all of that — the math doesn't work for them.

So we're shipping coexistence next month: same number, two interfaces. The agent's WhatsApp Business app stays intact (calls, groups, speed). The agency gains the centralized inbox with metrics and AI for 1:1 conversations. And likely permanently — because no third party can WhatsApp-call a customer from outside WhatsApp.

A month delay we hadn't planned. But the right call.
