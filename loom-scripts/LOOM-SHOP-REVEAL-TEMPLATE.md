# Shop Reveal Loom — Template for the 16 Mystery Shop Agencies

**Use this template** to record 16 individual Looms (one per shopped agency), personalized with each agency's specific response-time data from the April 16 batch.

**The ethical hook (this is what makes it work):**
Disclose the mystery shop **upfront**, then flip it into proof of the industry pain using the **97% stat**:
- 97% of real estate leads never convert to a sale (NAR)
- Leeren is literally one of the 97% — the fake inquiry is self-evidence of the problem
- "The issue isn't that I shopped you. The issue is this happens to you every day, just without the disclosure at the end."

Framing transparency as the product of value (not liability) is what makes these Looms land. Agencies who might have felt "tricked" now feel they got **honest data + a mirror + a solution** in one package.

---

## Meta

- **Duration target:** 2:30–3:30 (hard cap 3:30)
- **Language:** Match the language you used in the mystery shop (ES or EN)
- **Tone:** Transparent, warm, slightly self-deprecating. *"Yo fui literalmente uno de los 97%"* — own it. Not apologetic, not defensive.
- **Delivery:** WhatsApp to the SAME number used for the shop (the thread continues)
- **Timing:** Send **24–48 hours AFTER the shop**, not immediately. Let their close-loop settle. You arrive when the thread is quiet.
- **Camera:** ON (small bubble, bottom corner)
- **Screen share:** (1) your mystery-shop message screenshot, (2) simple 97/100 graphic, (3) Trochai inbox demo

## Why this approach works

1. **Transparency IS the product.** Agencies get pitched by SaaS constantly with hypothetical numbers. You're the one founder who shows up with *real data about their real setup* — and who owns the methodology instead of hiding it.

2. **The 97% stat flips the frame.** Instead of "sorry for tricking you," it becomes "I just proved to you what 97% of your daily inquiries look like. Here's how to stop losing your best time to them."

3. **The exit ramp builds trust even for the 'no' crowd.** If 2-3 of 16 say "don't contact me again," you respect it, delete, move on. That respect becomes a referral path in a small market like CR. Nobody tells their friends "that founder was respectful when I bounced."

4. **Costa Rica is small.** Word travels. This approach is designed so even the agencies that don't buy walk away thinking "that guy had integrity." That's the long-game play.
