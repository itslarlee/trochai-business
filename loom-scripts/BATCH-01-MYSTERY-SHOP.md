**Honest Loom framing (use this, not the "20/20 owners" line):**

Then per-prospect, use the SPECIFIC pattern that applies to THEIR agency (see per-agency context below).

### Insight 2 — Autoreply: only 1 of 20 confirmed

**Results Realty Group** is the only agency with a confirmed autoreply. The other 19: **unknown until the mystery shop reveals it**. Most likely 2-3 more will also have generic autoreplies, but we won't know until you shop them. Log autoreply presence + text when you do.

### Insight 3 — Fragmented contact within same brokerage

Multiple agencies (Realty ONE Group, KRAIN, RE/MAX Ocean Surf, Elite Properties, Propiedades Leitón) have agents or co-owners publishing their **own personal mobiles** separately. When a lead writes to Agent A who's in a showing, Agent B who's available doesn't even know the message exists. No shared inbox = lost context.

### Insight 4 — The "luxury" outlier

**2Costa Rica Real Estate doesn't use WhatsApp at all** — they use Zopim desktop live chat. For a 5-office luxury brokerage with $20M listings, that's an enormous wedge. See Special Case #21 below.
