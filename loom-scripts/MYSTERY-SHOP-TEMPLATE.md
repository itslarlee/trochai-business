# Mystery Shop Template — how to run it before each Loom

**Goal:** Generate real response-time data you can use in the Loom. Takes 2-3 min of agent time per agency. 100% ethical if you follow the rules below.

---

## Rules (non-negotiable)

1. **Use the AGENCY's public WhatsApp**, NOT the decision-maker's direct number from the CSV. You want to measure what a real buyer experiences, which is the main agency line (pulled from their website, Facebook page, or Google Business listing).
2. **One message only.** Don't schedule visits, don't commit to anything, don't keep the conversation going past 2 exchanges.
3. **When they reply,** respond ONCE within 10 minutes with: *"Perfecto, gracias. Sigo viendo opciones, le escribo si algo me calza."* Close the loop politely. Never ghost them.
4. **In the Loom, admit it.** *"Hice algo — el sábado le escribí a [Agency] haciéndome pasar por un comprador, para mostrarle lo que pasa."*
5. **Never name the agent who replied (or didn't).** Say "[Agency] took 4 hours" — never "your agent Juan took 4 hours". You're selling to the boss, not getting anyone in trouble.

---

## Timing — when to send the mystery shop

**Best send windows** (when the pain is most visible):
- **Saturday 2pm-6pm** — agents are with family, leads still write
- **Sunday 7pm-10pm** — classic "tire-kicker at night" moment
- **Weeknight 9pm-11pm** — international buyers in NYC/Miami/Toronto timezones

**Avoid:**
- Monday-Friday 9am-5pm (too fast a response, no pain to measure)

---

## The message (Spanish, ustedeo, realistic buyer voice)

Pick ONE template based on the agency's zone:

### For GAM agencies (Escazú, Santa Ana, San José)

> "Hola, buenas. Busco casa en [Escazú / Santa Ana / la zona que manejen], presupuesto alrededor de $[400K-500K], 3 habitaciones. ¿Qué tienen disponible?"

### For beach agencies (Tamarindo, Flamingo, Jacó, Nosara)

> "Hola, ando buscando casa en [Tamarindo / Jacó / zone], presupuesto $[500K-700K], 3 cuartos mínimo, cerca de la playa. ¿Qué me recomiendan?"

### For luxury-positioned agencies

> "Buenas. Estamos viendo opciones en [zone] por $[800K-1.2M], 4 cuartos, con buena vista. ¿Qué tienen?"

### For investment/condo-heavy agencies

> "Hola, busco una propiedad para renta vacacional en [zone], presupuesto $[250K-350K]. ¿Tienen algo que me sirva?"

## What to log (required — you'll use this in the Loom)

After you hit send, open `leads/master-leads.csv` (or a separate tracker) and log:

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| Agency | CasaMax |
| Message sent (timestamp) | Sat 2026-04-18 14:03 |
| Day of week + time of day | Saturday afternoon |
| First reply received | Sat 2026-04-18 18:14 (4h 11min) |
| Who replied | "An agent" — never a specific name |
| Reply quality | Generic / Tailored / With photos / Just asked questions back |
| Did they ask my budget again? | Yes/No (if yes, they're not reading the message carefully) |
| Autoreply? | Yes/No + contents if yes |

---

## Friday-afternoon batch ritual (recommended)

**Every Friday 3pm-5pm, batch-shop 5-10 agencies** for next week's Looms.

1. Pick 5-10 P1 leads you plan to pitch next week
2. For each, find the AGENCY main WhatsApp (from website, not CSV)
3. Send the appropriate message template
4. Log send timestamp
5. Come back Monday morning — log reply timestamps (or "no reply within 72h")
6. For each responder: send closing message *"Perfecto, gracias. Sigo viendo opciones..."*
7. Now you have Monday's / Tuesday's / Wednesday's Loom data ready

---

## When mystery shopping ISN'T possible

If the agency has no public WhatsApp (only a form, email, or phone), skip the shop for them. Use the **public-signal approach** instead in the Loom:

- Do they have WhatsApp Business (green checkmark) or regular WhatsApp?
- Do they have an autoreply set?
- Response-time badge on their WhatsApp profile ("Usually replies within...")?
- Is there a phone number listed but no WhatsApp?

Any of these is a hook: *"Alejandro, miré el WhatsApp de CasaMax — no tienen autoreply. Significa que un comprador que escriba a las 11pm no ve absolutamente nada hasta que alguien abra WhatsApp a las 7am."*

---

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Never schedule a property visit
- ❌ Never ask for a call
- ❌ Never send pictures of a fake ID / say you're from a specific country unless asked
- ❌ Never mystery-shop the same agency twice (once the data is in, you're done)
- ❌ Never share the specific conversation content in the Loom — just the timing data and quality summary
- ❌ Never mystery-shop AFTER sending the Loom — that contaminates your relationship
