## Cadence rules

- **Send window:** May 1–3 (right when the launch discount expires + new promo lands — the timing is the news).
- **One follow-up per recipient.** If no reply by May 8, drop. Don't badger — per `LOOM-OUTREACH-SYSTEM.md` the playbook is "respectful approach compounds in referral goodwill."
- **Order recipients by warmth:** Rodolfo first (warmest, expecting a follow-up), then any prospect who had partial engagement on the original Loom, then the cold ones.
- **Update `master-leads.csv` Status column the same day each follow-up sends.** Without this the next wave will be blind. Status values to use: `Followup-Sent-{YYYY-MM-DD}` or `Followup-Replied-{YYYY-MM-DD}`.

### C — "Lo veo más tarde / después" (polite stall)

Acknowledge gracefully. Add Q3 reminder to the calendar. Don't push.

> Sin problema, [don/doña {name}]. Cuando le calce, agendamos. Disfrute la semana.

### D — "No estoy interesado por ahora"

Accept and leave the door open.

> Sin ningún problema. Le agradezco la sinceridad. Si más adelante cambia el panorama, quedo a la orden. Muchas gracias.

### E — No reply by May 8

Drop. Move them to `dead-deals.md` with reason "no engagement on Loom + May follow-up." Don't follow up again — they've now had two messages with no response. The CR RE community is small; respect it.
