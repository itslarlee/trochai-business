# Trochai Content Marketing Strategy

Instagram + TikTok as primary outreach channels. No cold calling. Founder-led content.

---

## Why This Works for Trochai

- Your target audience (real estate agents in Costa Rica) lives on Instagram and WhatsApp
- Founder-led content is the #1 format for early-stage SaaS — it's authentic, cheap, and builds trust
- TikTok/Reels algorithm rewards content quality over follower count — you can go viral with 0 followers
- You're selling to people who already use social media daily for their listings
- A 60-second demo of Trochai replying to a WhatsApp message via AI is more convincing than any sales call

## How to Find Trending Audio

### In-App Methods (Free, do this daily)

**Instagram:**
1. Open Instagram → Create Reel → tap music icon → go to **"Trending"** tab → see top 50 tracks
2. While scrolling Reels, look for the **upward arrow** next to audio names — that means it's trending
3. Tap the audio name on any Reel to see how many Reels use it. **Sweet spot: 5,000-10,000 uses** (about to blow up, not oversaturated yet)
4. Follow **@creators** on Instagram — they post weekly trend reports with popular sounds

**TikTok:**
1. Go to **TikTok Creative Center** (ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter) → Trending → Songs → filter by your region
2. Use the **search bar** → type "trending sounds" or "viral audio" → tap "Sounds" tab
3. Scroll your FYP and notice which audio clips repeat across different creators

### Tools

| Tool | What it does | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| **TikTok Creative Center** | Official trending songs by region + time period | Free |
| **Instagram Trending tab** | Top 50 trending tracks in the Reels editor | Free |
| **[Tokchart](https://tokchart.com)** | Real-time trending TikTok sounds, charts + analytics | Free (basic), paid for full |
| **[HeyOrca blog](https://www.heyorca.com/blog/trending-audio-for-reels-tiktok)** | Weekly updated list of trending audio for Reels + TikTok | Free |
| **[Buffer trending sounds](https://buffer.com/resources/trending-songs-tiktok/)** | Monthly curated list with usage tips | Free |
| **[SocialPilot](https://www.socialpilot.co/blog/instagram-reels-trends)** | Weekly Instagram Reels trends + audio | Free |

### Strategy: The Cross-Platform Trick

Trending sounds move from TikTok → Instagram with a **3-7 day delay**. So:

1. Spot a sound blowing up on TikTok
2. Search for it on Instagram — if it has **fewer than 5,000 Reels**, you're early
3. Post your Reel with that audio **within 24-48 hours** of spotting it
4. Instagram's algorithm gives the strongest boost to early adopters of trending audio

### How to use trending audio for Trochai content

You don't need the audio to be about real estate. Use trending sounds as background for:
- **Text-on-screen** stat videos (the 5 stats above)
- **Before/after** transitions (WhatsApp chaos → Trochai organized)
- **POV videos** (relatable agent moments)
- **Screen recordings** with trending music underneath

The audio gets you reach. The content gets you followers. The product gets you customers.

## Video Production Tips

### Format that works

```
Hook (0-3 sec)     → "Your agency is losing leads right now"
Problem (3-8 sec)  → "Because no one responds at 11pm on a Saturday"
Solution (8-20 sec) → Show Trochai bot responding automatically
CTA (20-25 sec)    → "Link in bio" or "Follow for more"
```

### Production rules

- **Vertical always** (9:16 ratio)
- **Captions on every video** — 85% of social video is watched on mute
- **First 3 seconds are everything** — hook immediately, no intros or logos
- **15-45 seconds** is the sweet spot for Reels/TikTok
- **Natural lighting** > ring light > bad lighting
- **Phone camera is fine** — authenticity > production quality
- **Spanish first** (your market), English versions for wider reach
- **Use trending audio** when it fits — boosts algorithm reach
- **Screen recordings**: use a clean demo account, zoom into the important parts

## Video Editing Tools — Comparison & Recommendation

### Quick recommendation

| Need | Best tool | Cost |
|------|-----------|------|
| **Best overall for Reels/TikTok** | **CapCut** (desktop) | Free (Pro features now require subscription) |
| **Best free with no watermark** | **Instagram Edits** | Free, no watermark, 4K export |
| **Best for mobile-only editing** | **InShot** | $20 one-time purchase |
| **Best for auto-captions + talking head** | **Descript** | $24/mo |
| **Best for repurposing long videos into clips** | **Opus Clip** | Free tier, $19/mo for more |
| **Best professional (free)** | **DaVinci Resolve** | Free |

### Detailed breakdown

#### CapCut (Desktop + Mobile)
- **Best for**: Quick trend-based edits, auto captions, templates, effects
- **Pros**: Huge template library, auto-captions in Spanish, trending effects, exports without watermark (desktop), TikTok integration
- **Cons**: Moving to subscription model in 2026, some AI features now paywalled
- **Cost**: Free tier still usable, Pro ~$10/mo
- **Verdict**: Still the king for short-form content speed. Use the desktop app for no watermark.

#### Instagram Edits (Mobile)
- **Best for**: Creating Reels directly, seamless Instagram publishing
- **Pros**: Completely free, no watermark, 4K export, AI animation, built-in voice effects, shows real-time Reels stats
- **Cons**: New app so still buggy (crashes reported), no templates yet, Instagram-first (less flexible for TikTok)
- **Cost**: Free
- **Verdict**: Great if you're primarily posting to Instagram. Use alongside CapCut.

#### InShot (Mobile)
- **Best for**: Simple, fast mobile edits
- **Pros**: One-time $20 purchase (no subscription), intuitive, trimming + text + music + filters, great for Stories
- **Cons**: Less powerful than CapCut for effects/templates
- **Cost**: $20 lifetime
- **Verdict**: Best budget option. Reddit's #1 recommended CapCut alternative.

#### Descript (Desktop + Web)
- **Best for**: Talking-head videos, podcasts, transcript-based editing
- **Pros**: Edit video by editing text (delete a sentence from the transcript = deletes from video), auto-captions, AI filler word removal, screen recording
- **Cons**: Not great for trend-based content or effects, subscription only
- **Cost**: Free tier (limited), $24/mo
- **Verdict**: Use if you do lots of talking-head "founder journey" content. Edit by text is magic.

#### Opus Clip (Web)
- **Best for**: Turning one long video into 10+ short clips automatically
- **Pros**: AI finds "viral moments", auto-formats for vertical, adds captions, batch processing
- **Cons**: Only for repurposing (can't create from scratch), quality varies
- **Cost**: Free (3 clips), $19/mo
- **Verdict**: Use when you record a 10-min demo and want to chop it into 8 Reels automatically.

#### DaVinci Resolve (Desktop)
- **Best for**: Professional editing, color grading, complex projects
- **Pros**: Hollywood-grade tool, completely free, no watermark
- **Cons**: Steep learning curve, overkill for quick social content
- **Cost**: Free (Studio version $295, not needed)
- **Verdict**: Skip for now. Only if you eventually want cinematic brand videos.

#### Remotion (Terminal — Claude Code)
- **Best for**: Programmatic, templated, data-driven video generation at scale
- **Pros**: Create 100 video variants from 1 template, multiple formats (9:16, 1:1, 16:9) in one go, brand consistency enforced by code, React/TypeScript stack, free for < 3 employees, official Claude Code skills (37 rule files)
- **Cons**: Requires React knowledge, not for editing real footage or talking-head content, no visual timeline editor
- **Cost**: Free (Trochai qualifies), AWS Lambda rendering ~$0.01/min
- **Verdict**: Game-changer for templated content — stat reveal videos, product demo animations, property listing Reels, batch ad creatives. Use alongside CapCut for filmed content. See **[VIBE-MARKETING.md](./VIBE-MARKETING.md)** for full setup guide.

### My recommendation for you

**Start with this combo:**

1. **CapCut (desktop)** — your main editor for filmed Reels/TikToks. Templates, auto-captions, effects.
2. **Instagram Edits (phone)** — for quick on-the-go edits and direct Reel publishing.
3. **Remotion (terminal)** — for templated/animated content: stat videos, product demos, property listings, ad creatives at scale.
4. **Opus Clip** (later) — when you have longer demo recordings to chop up.

This gives you everything you need at essentially $0/month. Remotion handles the programmatic/batch content, CapCut handles the filmed/edited content.

## Growth Tactics

### Week 1-4: Launch phase
- Post first batch of 10-15 pre-made videos
- Follow 200-300 real estate agents/agencies in Costa Rica
- Like and comment genuinely on their posts (not spam)
- Add "trochai.com" to bio with a clear one-liner
- Use relevant hashtags in Spanish

### Week 5-8: Consistency phase
- Maintain 4-5 posts/week schedule
- Analyze which content types get most engagement
- Double down on what works
- Start engaging in DMs with people who comment (not selling, just chatting)
- Create response videos to common questions

### Week 9-12: Conversion phase
- Share early results/testimonials if you have them
- Create a "link in bio" page (Linktree or similar) with demo booking link
- Start posting client wins / social proof
- Consider boosting top-performing Reels ($5-10/day on Instagram)

### Ongoing
- Repurpose: every Reel goes to TikTok and vice versa
- Every carousel can become a Reel and vice versa
- Monitor DMs — that's where deals happen
- Track which content drives actual website visits (UTM links in bio)
