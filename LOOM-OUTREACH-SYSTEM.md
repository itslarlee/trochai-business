## The Core Insight

Every video in the transcript says the same thing differently:

**Personalized video outreach to a warm audience converts 10-30x better than cold text.**

The system has 3 phases:
1. **Warm first** — they know your face before you pitch
2. **Demo the outcome** — show, don't tell
3. **Follow up relentlessly** — the money is in the follow-ups

For Trochai specifically, we have a weapon most SaaS founders don't: **we can demo the product live in 30 seconds.** Send a WhatsApp message to a Trochai demo number loaded with the prospect's properties → watch the AI respond instantly with property details, photos, availability. That's Frankie's "demonstrate the outcome" on steroids.

## Phase 1: Build the Warm Audience (Week 1-2)

### LinkedIn Connection Blitz

**Goal:** Add 100 CR real estate professionals on LinkedIn in 3 days.

**Who to add:**
- Agency owners / managing brokers (primary — these are buyers)
- Operations managers (secondary — they influence the purchase)
- Top-producing agents (tertiary — they feel the WhatsApp pain most)

**How:**
1. Go to LinkedIn Sales Navigator (or regular search)
2. Filters: Location = Costa Rica, Industry = Real Estate, Title contains: "director", "gerente", "broker", "corredor", "inmobiliario", "bienes raíces", "propiedades", "real estate"
3. Send connection requests in batches of 20-30/day (LinkedIn limits ~100/week)
4. **No pitch in the connection request.** Blank or one line max:
   - Blank (highest acceptance rate)
   - Or: "Hola [Nombre], vi que trabaja en bienes raíces en [zona]. Me encantaría conectar."

**Why this works (Frankie):** "They think they found you rather than you strategically reaching out. It changes the power dynamic."

### Hand-Raiser Posts (LinkedIn)

Once you have 50+ RE connections, start posting. These are NOT product posts — they're "offer summaries" that get your ICP to raise their hand.

**Post frequency:** 2-3 per week, in addition to your regular daily LinkedIn content.

**The formula:** State the promise (not the service) + ask who's interested.

#### Hand-Raiser Post Templates (Spanish)

**Template 1 — The Promise Post:**

> He estado trabajando con agencias inmobiliarias en Costa Rica y noté algo interesante:
>
> La mayoría pierde leads porque tarda horas en responder por WhatsApp. No es culpa de nadie — WhatsApp Business no fue diseñado para equipos.
>
> Estoy preparando algo que responde a cada lead en menos de 30 segundos, 24/7, con información real de sus propiedades.
>
> Si maneja una agencia y le interesaría ver cómo funciona, comente "me interesa" y le grabo un video personalizado con un ejemplo usando propiedades de su zona.

**Template 2 — The Question Post:**

> Pregunta para agentes inmobiliarios en Costa Rica:
>
> ¿Cuántos mensajes de WhatsApp del fin de semana pasado siguen sin respuesta en su agencia?
>
> No es retórico — genuinamente me interesa el número. Estoy investigando cuánto revenue se pierde por tiempo de respuesta en WhatsApp.

**Template 3 — The Data Post:**

> 917 minutos.
>
> Eso es lo que tarda en promedio un agente inmobiliario en responder un lead nuevo. Más de 15 horas.
>
> El 78% de los compradores compra con el primer agente que responde.
>
> Si maneja una agencia y quiere ver cuánto le está costando eso en comisiones perdidas, le puedo grabar un video de 3 minutos mostrándole los números. Comente "quiero ver" abajo.

**Template 4 — The Before/After Post:**

> Ayer hice una prueba:
>
> Mandé un mensaje de WhatsApp a 10 agencias inmobiliarias en el GAM preguntando por una propiedad.
>
> → 3 respondieron en menos de una hora
> → 4 respondieron al día siguiente
> → 3 no respondieron nunca
>
> Ahora imagínese que ese mensaje lo mandara un comprador con presupuesto listo. ¿A cuál de esas 10 agencias cree que le compra?
>
> Estoy construyendo algo para resolver exactamente esto. Si le interesa ver un demo con propiedades de su zona, comente abajo.

**Key rules for hand-raiser posts:**
- Promise, not service. "Responder en 30 segundos" not "herramienta de IA para WhatsApp"
- Always end with a specific CTA: "comente X abajo"
- When someone comments → send them a Loom (Phase 2)
- Don't overthink it. Post, see what gets traction, iterate. "People have short attention spans on social media. They won't remember that you're the same guy who posted something else 10 minutes ago." (Frankie)

### Social Engagement (Parallel)

While you're posting, engage with your new connections' content:

- **Like** 5-10 ICP posts per day
- **Comment** on 3-5 posts with something substantive (not "great post!")
- **Share** an agency's listing with your take on the market
- This takes 15-20 min/day
- After 1-2 weeks, they recognize your face and name when the Loom arrives

## Phase 2: The Loom Video

This is the core weapon. Two types:

### Type A: Personalized Loom (3-5 min)

Send to high-priority prospects or anyone who commented on a hand-raiser post.

**Before recording:**
1. Open their website in a browser tab
2. Open their Instagram or Facebook in another tab
3. Open their WhatsApp number (if public) in another tab
4. Find one specific thing to comment on (a listing, a post, a stat, their response time)
5. Have Trochai demo ready with 2-3 of their actual properties loaded (or properties from their zone)

**Recording setup:**
- Loom (free plan works, paid gives you CTA buttons and analytics)
- Camera on, mic on, screen sharing their website
- Name the video: "Video para [Nombre] — [Agency Name]" (they see this in the thumbnail)
- Smile. Be human. Add humor if natural.

#### The Script (ADA Framework — adapted)

**A — Attention (20-30 sec):**
Show their website/Instagram on screen. Smile. Wave.

> "Hola [Nombre], soy Leeren. Estaba viendo el sitio de [Agency] y me llamó la atención [something specific — their listing count, a property, their zones, their reviews, their response time if you tested it]."

Add a personal touch or humor:
> "Vi que cubren desde Santa Ana hasta Ciudad Colón — eso es bastante terreno para un equipo."

**I — Interest / Quick Insight (30-45 sec):**
Give them something useful. This is the "quick fix" — something they can see about their own setup that's relevant to what you do.

Options:
- "Noté que su sitio tiene un botón de WhatsApp pero cuando se hace clic a las 10pm, ¿quién responde?"
- "Vi que tienen [X] propiedades listadas. Si un comprador manda un mensaje preguntando por la casa en [zona], ¿cuánto tarda en promedio en recibir respuesta?"
- "Probé mandarles un mensaje por WhatsApp ayer a las 8pm. [Describe what happened — did they respond? How long did it take?]"

> "No digo esto como crítica — el 47% de las agencias tiene el mismo reto. WhatsApp Business no fue diseñado para equipos."

**D — Desire / Demo (60-90 sec):**
This is where you show the outcome. Switch screen to the Trochai demo.

> "Le quiero mostrar algo. Voy a mandarle un mensaje de WhatsApp a este número como si fuera un comprador interesado en una propiedad en [their zone]."

*Type a WhatsApp message:* "Hola, vi una casa en Santa Ana de 3 habitaciones. ¿Tienen algo disponible por $300K?"

*Wait for Trochai AI to respond — it should respond in seconds with matching properties, photos, details.*

> "Eso es lo que su cliente vería. En menos de 30 segundos. A las 2 de la mañana, un domingo. Sin que nadie de su equipo tenga que estar despierto."

If you have it, show one more message — a follow-up question from the "buyer" to show the AI handles conversation context.

**Show proof (15 sec):**
> "Los datos dicen que responder en 5 minutos en vez de 30 le da 21 veces más probabilidad de calificar el lead. Y el 78% de los compradores compra con el primero que responde."

**A — Action / CTA (15-20 sec):**
Low friction. Don't ask for a call — ask for a reply.

> "Esto cuesta $99 al mes. Lo que hace es responder a cada lead de WhatsApp automáticamente con información de sus propiedades — 24/7. Si su equipo cierra un solo deal adicional al año, se paga solo como 100 veces.
>
> Si le interesa, simplemente respóndame a este mensaje con un 'sí' y le configuro un demo personalizado con propiedades de [their zone]. Se conecta en 5 minutos."

**Total time:** ~3-4 minutes.

#### Key Recording Tips

- **Name the video file** with their name — they see it in the preview
- **First 3 seconds matter most** — smile, wave, say their name. The Loom thumbnail/GIF shows the first few seconds
- **Don't be perfect.** A slightly rough, authentic video beats a polished one. You're a founder, not a salesperson
- **Show their stuff on screen** — website, listings, Instagram. This creates curiosity ("why is this guy looking at my website?")
- **Never say "this is not a sales call."** It paradoxically makes it feel like one
- **Keep it under 5 minutes.** 3 is ideal. "Attention is limited, shorter is better." (Rafa)

### Type B: Evergreen Loom (for scale)

Once you've sent 10-15 personalized Looms and know the common objections, record ONE master video that works for everyone.

**When to switch:** After you hear the same 2-3 objections repeatedly and know how to address them. Common objections for Trochai will likely be:
- "¿Y si la IA responde mal?" → Show the human handoff feature
- "Ya probamos algo parecido y no funcionó" → Show how Trochai is different (property-aware, not generic)
- "Suena caro" → Contextualize: one closed deal = $7,500 commission. Tool costs $99/month
- "No tenemos tiempo para configurarlo" → Show the 5-min setup

**Evergreen video structure (5 min):**
1. Hook: "Si maneja una agencia inmobiliaria en Costa Rica, este video le va a ahorrar mucho dinero"
2. Problem: The 917-minute stat. 47% no response. 78% buy from first responder
3. Mistakes agencies make: (a) Relying on one person to check WhatsApp, (b) No coverage nights/weekends, (c) No follow-up system
4. Demo: Live WhatsApp conversation with Trochai AI
5. Price + contextualization: "$99/mes. Un deal cerrado = $7,500 en comisión. Se paga 75 veces"
6. How to buy: "Responda este mensaje o haga clic en el botón de abajo"
7. Objection handling: Address the top 2-3 objections in 30 seconds

**Personalization layer:** The video is evergreen, but the MESSAGE you send it with is personalized:

> "Hola [Nombre], vi que [Agency] maneja propiedades en [zona]. Grabé este video explicando cómo algunas agencias están respondiendo leads de WhatsApp en menos de 30 segundos — creo que le puede servir. [link]"

## Phase 3: The Outreach Sequence

### For Hand-Raiser Responders (they commented on your post)

These are WARM. They already expressed interest.

| Day | Channel | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|
| 0 | LinkedIn | They comment on your post |
| 0 | LinkedIn DM | "Gracias por el comentario, [Nombre]. Le voy a grabar un video rápido con un ejemplo para [Agency/zona]. Se lo mando en las próximas horas." |
| 0-1 | LinkedIn DM | Send personalized Loom link. Message: "Aquí está, [Nombre]. Puse propiedades de [zona] como ejemplo. Dura 3 minutos — véalo cuando pueda y me dice qué le parece." |
| 3 | LinkedIn DM | If no reply: "¿Tuvo chance de ver el video? Si tiene alguna pregunta, con gusto." |
| 5 | WhatsApp | If you have their number: Send voice note (30 sec). "Hola [Nombre], soy Leeren. Le mandé un video por LinkedIn sobre cómo responder leads de WhatsApp más rápido. ¿Lo pudo ver? Si no, se lo reenvío por acá." |
| 7 | LinkedIn DM | Send a value piece: a stat, a tip, a relevant article. No pitch. "Vi este dato y me acordé de nuestra conversación: [insight]." |
| 14 | LinkedIn or WhatsApp | Final follow-up: "Hola [Nombre], no quiero ser insistente — solo quería saber si le interesa ver cómo funciona en vivo con propiedades de su zona. Si no es buen momento, todo bien." |

### For Cold Outreach (no prior engagement)

These need MORE warming. Don't send Loom on day 1.

| Day | Channel | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|
| 0 | LinkedIn | Send connection request (blank or minimal message) |
| 1-7 | LinkedIn | Like/comment on 2-3 of their posts. Be visible |
| 7 | LinkedIn DM | Once connected: Warm message — NOT a pitch. "Hola [Nombre], vi que [Agency] cubre [zona]. Estoy investigando cómo las agencias en CR manejan los leads por WhatsApp. ¿Usan WhatsApp Business o algún otro sistema?" |
| 8-9 | LinkedIn DM | Based on their reply, continue conversation. Learn about their setup |
| 10 | LinkedIn DM | Send personalized Loom: "Basado en lo que me contó, grabé algo que creo le puede servir. Dura 3 minutos. [link]" |
| 12 | WhatsApp | If no reply: Voice note or text with Loom link |
| 15 | LinkedIn | Value piece (stat, article, tip) |
| 21 | LinkedIn or WhatsApp | Final follow-up |

### For WhatsApp-First Outreach (you have their number, no LinkedIn)

Some CR agencies are WhatsApp-native and barely use LinkedIn. Go direct.

| Day | Channel | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|

| 3 | WhatsApp | Voice note (30 sec): Recap the key insight from the video. "Hola [Nombre], soy Leeren. En el video muestro cómo responder a un lead de WhatsApp en menos de 30 segundos con información de sus propiedades. Si le interesó, me dice y le configuro un demo en vivo." |

## Phase 4: Closing (No Sales Call)

This is Frankie's approach. Once someone is interested, you DON'T book a sales call. You send them a "closing video" (can be evergreen or personalized) that includes:

1. **The full demo** — show Trochai working with properties from their zone
2. **Price contextualization** — "Son $99/mes. Sus agentes manejan leads que valen $7,500+ en comisión. Si Trochai ayuda a cerrar un solo deal adicional al año, el retorno es 75x."
3. **Exactly how to buy** — "Si quiere empezar, esto es lo que sigue: (1) Haga clic en este link, (2) Conecte su WhatsApp Business en 5 minutos, (3) Su agente de IA empieza a responder hoy. El primer mes tiene 50% de descuento con el código TROCHAILAUNCH."
4. **Objection handling** — address the top 3 objections preemptively

> "Uh, we actually don't do sales calls and I haven't for 5 years. We actually close all our stuff in 5-minute videos." — Frankie
>
> "Clients actually prefer to be sold that way because they don't have to schedule. They don't have to waste time." — Frankie
>
> "You also save yourself an hour of talking to people who are broke." — Frankie

### The Only Question You'll Get

Frankie says the main question is always: "I know you showed that example in [place], but I'm in [different place]. Does it still work for me?"

For Trochai: "Yo estoy en Guanacaste, no en el GAM. ¿Funciona igual?"
Answer: "Sí. Trochai funciona con cualquier propiedad en cualquier zona de Costa Rica. Solo necesita un número de WhatsApp Business y sus listados."

## Daily Routine

This system works if you follow a consistent daily routine. Budget 1-2 hours/day.

### Morning (30 min) — Engage

- [ ] Check LinkedIn notifications — respond to all comments on your posts
- [ ] Like/comment on 5-10 ICP posts in your feed
- [ ] Accept new connection requests
- [ ] Reply to any Loom responses from yesterday

### Midday (45 min) — Record

- [ ] Research 3 prospects (10 min each — check their website, Instagram, listings)
- [ ] Record 3 personalized Loom videos (3-5 min each)
- [ ] Send each Loom via the appropriate channel (LinkedIn DM or WhatsApp)

### Afternoon (15 min) — Follow Up

- [ ] Check Loom analytics — who watched but didn't reply?
- [ ] Send follow-up messages to day 3/5/7 prospects
- [ ] Send voice notes on WhatsApp for high-priority prospects who watched but didn't reply

### Weekly (30 min) — Post + Plan

- [ ] Write and publish 2-3 hand-raiser posts for the week
- [ ] Review metrics dashboard
- [ ] Adjust sequence/scripts based on what's working
- [ ] Add new prospects to the pipeline

## Scaling: From Custom to Evergreen

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-3): All Custom
- Record personalized Looms for every prospect
- Learn what resonates, what objections come up
- Track reply rates per script variation

### Phase 2 (Weeks 4-6): Hybrid
- Record ONE evergreen video that handles the common objections
- Use it for Tier B/C prospects and high-volume outreach
- Still record custom Looms for Tier A prospects (top 20 agencies)

### Phase 3 (Weeks 7+): System
- Evergreen video for most outreach
- Personalized text message accompanying the evergreen video
- Custom Looms only for strategic accounts (large agencies, referral sources)
- Hand-raiser posts driving inbound → evergreen video → close

> "When it's a brand new offer and I haven't had somebody say yes to it, I send a custom video. When I get used to people saying yes, then I just one video to rule them all." — Frankie
