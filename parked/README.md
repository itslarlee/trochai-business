## Why here and not deleted

These are real strategic memos worth preserving. If/when we decide to build them, the research is done and we can ship faster. But mixing them with active docs creates clutter.

## When to un-park

- **Voice agent**: when we have 10+ paying customers asking for voice calls, OR when a specific enterprise deal needs it
- **Mobile app**: when agent-on-the-go becomes a real bottleneck for existing customers (likely after 50+ agencies onboarded)

Until then — read them once, don't act on them.
