# Video Scripts — Stat Series

6 short-form videos for Instagram Reels / TikTok. Each one punchy, uses a real stat, shows how Trochai solves it, and cites the source.

## Source Accuracy Check

Before filming, here's what I verified:

| Stat | Accuracy | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **2-3% leads convert** | Roughly accurate | NAR says 0.4-1.2% baseline. Real Geeks says with good follow-up it can reach 2-3%. Safe to say "less than 3%" or "only 2-3% at best" |
| **78% choose first to respond** | Commonly cited, slightly fuzzy source | NAR 2025 says 67-76% of buyers only interviewed ONE agent. The "78% first to respond" stat is widely used in real estate marketing but originates from Lead Connect / older data. Still directionally true and commonly accepted. |
| **62% leads arrive after hours** | Accurate | Sourced from NAR + Zillow Group research, cited by EnvisionSFC |
| **48% never follow up / 80% need 5+ touches** | Accurate | Directly from SPOTIO (actually 5-12 follow-ups) |
| **40% of CR buyers are foreign** | Accurate | Coldwell Banker Costa Rica + TheLatinvestor 2025: "foreign buyers now account for nearly 40% of all transactions" |

### Language support in Trochai
The bot currently supports **Spanish and English** as response languages (set per organization). However, since it runs on GPT, it **understands messages in ANY language** — Chinese, French, German, etc. It just responds in the org's configured language. So if a Chinese buyer writes in Mandarin, the bot understands the intent and responds in Spanish/English. The agent can also see the original message and use external translation if needed.

For the video, lean into "our AI understands your clients no matter what language they speak" rather than "we respond in their language" — because technically it responds in your org's language.

### About 你干嘛
**Not a bad word.** It's informal Chinese for "what are you doing?" / "what's up?" It can sound annoyed depending on tone but it's NOT offensive, NOT a swear word. It's actually a popular TikTok meme sound. Totally safe to use. It's casual/playful, which is perfect for the tone of the video.

### Video 4: "50+ horas al mes en preguntas repetidas"

**Duration:** 20-25 seconds
**Vibe:** Clone effect chaos → calm punchline
**Audio:** Upbeat or comedic trending sound
**Effect:** CapCut clone/body duplication effect — multiple yous on screen at once

**How to film for clone effect:**
Film ALL question takes from the **same static camera position** (tripod or propped phone). Same background. Don't move the camera. You move to different positions in the frame for each take. CapCut's clone effect layers them together so it looks like 5-7 versions of you asking at the same time.

```
[STATIC FRAME — Home desk or living room, tripod, good lighting]

[Clones appear one by one, each asking a question, overlapping]

Clone 1 (left side, standing): "Está disponible?"
Clone 2 (right side, sitting): "Tiene parqueo?"
Clone 3 (center, leaning in): "Acepta mascotas?"
Clone 4 (far left, from behind): "IS IT AVAILABLE?"
Clone 5 (far right, on the floor): "Cuánto es el depósito?"
Clone 6 (popping in from corner): "Está amueblado?"
Clone 7 (behind clone 1): "Está disponible??"

[All clones talking over each other — chaos builds for 5-8 seconds]

[HARD CUT — silence]

[Solo vos, relajado, pies arriba, mismo lugar pero sin clones]

"No. No estoy disponible."

[Pausa]

"Pero mi bot sí. 24/7."

"Según MindStudio, sus agentes gastan más de 50 horas al mes
respondiendo las mismas preguntas."

"Déjenselo al bot. Usen su tiempo en lo que importa."

"Fuente: MindStudio."
```

**Production notes:**
- TRIPOD IS ESSENTIAL — camera cannot move between takes for clone effect to work
- Film each "clone" separately: same background, you just stand in a different spot each time
- CapCut clone tutorial: record → overlay → mask each layer → align timeline
- The chaos building up is what makes the hard cut to silence + punchline land
- Contrast: chaos (clones yelling) → calm (just you, relaxed, deadpan)
- Film 7-8 takes to have options — you can use 5-7 in final edit
- Try different energies: one whispers, one yells, one is annoyed, one is desperate

- The shift from silly diving to serious face is what makes the punchline land
- "Vos no podés mandar 5 mensajes?" is the closer — deliver it deadpan
- Historical context: Greece match was Round of 16, Costa Rica had a red card (10 men), Navas was named Man of the Match
