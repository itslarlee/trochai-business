## Engagement Strategy Notes

### High-Priority Connections (engage weekly)
1. **RE/MAX, Coldwell Banker, KW Costa Rica** company pages -- comment on their posts
2. **Sarah Breitlander, Richard Bexon, Lindsey Cantillo** -- engage with their content, they are CR RE opinion leaders
3. **Leadsales, Respond.io, Kommo, Wati** -- follow their content strategy as competitors
4. **Carlos Munoz 4S, Andrea Rodriguez Valdez** -- LatAm RE thought leaders with massive reach

### Content Engagement Approach
- Comment meaningfully on posts from the people/companies above (not generic "great post!")
- Share free tools and insights about WhatsApp for real estate (not sales pitches)
- Reference CR real estate market data when engaging with international proptech founders
- Post in Spanish for CR/LatAm audience, English for international proptech/SaaS audience

### Connection Request Templates
- **For CR agents/brokers:** Reference specific CR market knowledge; mention you're building tools for their workflow
- **For PropTech founders:** Reference their company's approach; position as fellow LatAm proptech founder
- **For WhatsApp ecosystem:** Reference shared space; position as vertical-specific (real estate) vs. horizontal
- **For SaaS/VC:** Reference CR tech ecosystem; position Trochai as CR-based, LatAm-focused SaaS
